# Barbecue Sauces Marinade

## Ingredients

* 1.5 cup salad oil
* 1 tbsp black pepper
* 3/4 cup soy sauce
* 1/2 cup wine vinegar
* 1/4 cup worcestershire sauce
* 1 1/2 tsp parsley
* 2 tbsp dry mustard
* 2 garlic cloves
* 2 1/4 tsp salt
* 1/3 cup fresh lemon juice

## Directions

Mix all ingredients well
