# Cream Soup Substitute

## Ingredients

* 2 tbsp butter
* 3 tbsp flour
* 0.5 cup chicken broth
* 0.5 cup milk
* Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Melt butter in a sauce pan
2. Whisk in flour
3. Slowly stir in the chicken broth, and then the milk
4. Whisk until thickened
5. Salt and pepper to taste
