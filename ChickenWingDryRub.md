# Chicken Wing Dry Rub

## Notes

For 2 pounds of chicken wings

## Ingredients

* 60g all purpose flour
* 2 tsp brown sugar
* 1 tsp smoked paprika
* 1 tsp chili powder
* 0.5 tsp garlic powder
* 0.5 tsp onion powder
* Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together
2. Coat chicken wings
