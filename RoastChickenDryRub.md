# Roast Chicken Dry Rub

## Ingredients

* 0.5 tsp sweet paprika
* 0.5 tsp cumin
* 0.5 tsp coriander
* 0.5 tsp allspice
* 0.25 tsp cinnamon
* 0.25 tsp nutmeg
* 0.25 tsp cloves
* 0.25 tsp cardamom
* 0.25 tsp turmeric

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together
2. Coat chicken
