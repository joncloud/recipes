# Asian Beef Marinade

## Ingredients

* 0.25 cup low sodium soy sauce
* 4 cloves garlic, minced
* 1 tbsp sesame oil
* 2 tbsp brown sugar
* 1 tbsp rice wine vinegar
* 1 tbsp ginger root
* 1 tsp chili garlic sauce
* 1 tbsp cornstarch

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together
2. Marinade beef for at least 20 minutes
